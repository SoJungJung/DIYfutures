Welcome to Rickrolling 103

What is Rickrolling?

Rickrolling is an internet meme and prank involving an unexpected appearance of the music video for "Never Gonna Give You Up," a 1987 hit song by British singer Rick Astley. The prank is often carried out by sharing a link that one would expect to be relevant to the topic at hand, which instead leads to Astley's music video.

How It Works

Typically, a person is "rickrolled" when they are tricked into clicking a link that leads to the music video. The link is often disguised as something else, making the surprise more effective.
