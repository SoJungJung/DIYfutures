Finanicial Plans

South Korea's Ministry of Defense has partnered with banks to offer a savings product called the "Soldier's Tomorrow Future Savings Account" to military personnel.

Soldier's Tomorrow Future Savings Account offers high interest rates (around 5% from the bank + 1% from the government), a social rehabilitation reserve (71% of the principal), and tax-free benefits, and I can expect to receive around $12,000 at maturity.

I'll put in all the money I can put in.
