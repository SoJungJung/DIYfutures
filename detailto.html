Finanicial Plans

South Korea's Ministry of Defense has partnered with banks to offer a savings product called the "Soldier's Tomorrow Future Savings Account" to military personnel.

The Soldier's Tomorrow Future Savings Account offers high interest rates (around 5% from the bank + 1% from the government), tax-free benefits, and when I finish my service, I can expect to receive around $12,000 at maturity.

I'll put in all the money I can put in.
